Hello and Welcome

Hi, my name is Ian and this is my blog. Here, I will talk about my game development experiences and share some of my knoeledge.


I stream my proccess on twitch sometimes. There is no official stream schedule yet but one will be set up in the near future. Feel free to follow me to catch my streams!


I program mainly in c++ and I am learning Javascript as well. For games I am learning to use the unreal engine, but I may also try other engines to see if there is one I like better. I aim to complete every aspect of a game by myself, from art to game design to music.


The main reason I am publishing this blog is because I am finding myself lost in laziness often. I hope that with this blog I can find motivation to work through my laziness and finally accomplish something. On top of making games, I also constantly challenge myself with other projects, like working through daily programming challenges or this blog, which is all coded from scratch without the use of any other frameworks.
