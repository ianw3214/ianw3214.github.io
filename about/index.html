About Me

I'm Ian Wang, a third year computer science student at the University of Toronto. As an avid game developer, I'm always trying my best to learn new skills and techniques to improve my craft.

I started this blog mainly to help myself learn certain concepts better, to practice my writing skills, and to share some of my experiences in gamedev or just programming in general. I will be writing mostly about gamedev topics, but I will also include some general programming posts. I personally love C++, and I'm hoping to make some posts about C++ topics as well.

You can find some of the games I've made on itch.io or github. If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions, shoot me a message on twitter or email me at ianianw21@gmail.com.
